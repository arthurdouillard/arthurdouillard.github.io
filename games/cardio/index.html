How it works

Your body is the controller

A lightweight pose model runs locally in your browser. It tracks body landmarks—not your image—to estimate your lane, detect a squat, and count alternating steps. Lift your knees in a clear left-right rhythm to accelerate; the road motion and speedometer respond to your cadence.
